Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide urgent assignment help? +

Yes, we do! We understand deadlines are critical. Urgent assignment help is available, but please note that this is subject to our current workload and tutor availability. We recommend contacting us via WhatsApp for the quickest response.

Can books be rented monthly? +

Absolutely. We offer flexible book rental plans, typically on a monthly basis, for expensive reference and academic books. Visit the store or call us to check the availability and deposit requirements for the book you need.

Are instant doubt classes available daily? +

Yes, we generally provide instant doubt classes daily during our operating hours. However, this is always subject to the availability of our expert tutors. We recommend calling ahead or checking our daily schedule before visiting for a specific subject.
